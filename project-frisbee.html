Rules of Ultimate Frisbee

  • Teams and Field
    • Two teams play on a rectangular field with an end zone at each end.
  • Objective
    • Score by completing a pass in the opposing team's end zone.
  • Passing and Defense
    • The disc holder cannot run; drops and interceptions change possession.
  • Spirit of the Game
    • Players self-officiate and share responsibility for fair play.
